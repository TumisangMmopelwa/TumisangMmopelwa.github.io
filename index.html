Our Story

The day we first met 26.09.2015
We first met on the 26th of September 2015,the last day of the Randy Skeete crusade which was held at Independence Arena in Windhoek.We met after divine Service when a friend, Davey called Steve to come and greet someone(Kgala). The moment I shaked Kgala's hand I said to myself "I got a wife!" not knowing that, that was also what was in Kgala's mind!
Our First Date 29.09.2015
On the 29th of September(Tuesday) we went to the museum,show ground and the mall where we had lunch at Pizza place,while in Pizza Place we begun to share our life stories, we later went to the zoo.While at the show grounds we entered a a competition to travel to south Africa.
Our First Holiday October 2015
While at the show grounds on our first date we entered a competition to travel to south Africa.A few weeks after entering the competition we won a trip to go to any place in the Free State in South Africa...
The Proposal October 2015
In October the same year,Steve proposed and Kgala accepted Steve's proposal.Later in December Steve travelled to Kanye to meet Kgala's family and their love continued to blossom.
Engagement 03.05.2016
At the end of January,Steve went back to Kanye to see Kgala's mother and asked her to arrage the date for the Lobola so he could marry Kgala.Kgala,s mom arranged everything and after 8 months on courtship Kgala and Steve officially engaged on the 3rd of May 2016.
